About

The Precinct Bar & Restaurant is dedicated to producing high quality products using fresh and local produce and delivering high quality customer service. We have created a casual cafe and restaurant where people are transported from their normal lives into a vibrant, busy, eclectic yet intimate establishment showcasing our love for great coffee and delicious food. The Precinct is all about tapping into the localities urban grit right in the heart of East Victoria Park’s café and dining scene.

So sit back, relax, and enjoy your stay at The Precinct.
